Getting Started

Welcome to the Stationery Management System! Here's how to get started:

  1. Navigate to the Dashboard to see an overview of all classes
  2. Click on any class card to view student details
  3. Add new students using the "Add Student" button
  4. Track purchases and balances for each student
  5. Generate reports to analyze financial data

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I add a new student?

A: Go to the Students view and click "Add New Student", or from within a class detail view.


Q: How can I track purchases?

A: When adding or editing a student, enter the purchased items and amount spent.


Q: What do the balance and dues represent?

A: Balance shows the remaining amount a student can spend, while dues show any outstanding payments.
